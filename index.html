A Simple And Fun Platform Fighter For Everyone

Welcome! We are Punch Studios, we have all played the Super Smash Bros. series and understand how fun it can be, but it also can be hard to pick up. Here at Punch studios are goal is a make an simple, yet fun, approachable platform fighter that anyone can play!

Smash Town is a free to play PC Game that is designed to have a modern pixel art feel. We've definitely simplifed a lot from Super Smash Bros., so if you're coming from there you'll definitely notice a difference. But if not, the controls are still complex enough to do awesome tricks.
