Coverage

We here at Smash Studios have made it our goal to create a simplified platform fighter that anyone can pick up and have fun immediately.

If you ever played smash before, you know that it can take years to become the least bit proficient at it. This immediate barrier can be intimidating for newer players and can make it very hard to play. In Smash Town, we have simplified many of the mechanics removed other's entirely.

The game is in very early development right now, and only an alpha version has been released. If you want to try it out just download and have fun!
